Welcome to weatherbot documentation
====================================

Description:
============

Welcome to your personal weather bot. Stay dry and prosper, human.  

Features:
 • Current Conditions: Get your current conditions and temperature in a kik.
 • Forecasts: Choose between 3- and 7-day forecasts. For more details, you can always click through to The Weather Channel.
 • Daily forecast alert: Customize when you get a daily forecast--and avoid getting caught in the rain!
